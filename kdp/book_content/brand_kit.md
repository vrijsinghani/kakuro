# Brand Kit: Kakuro Puzzle Book

## Identity

**Voice:** The Patient Puzzle Guide | Brain Wellness Advocate

**Bio:** One puzzle at a time | Making logic accessible | Your brain's favorite workout | Screen-free satisfaction

**Signature Phrases:**
- "One puzzle at a time"
- "Happy solving!"
- "That's it."

---

## Brand Essence

Patient teacher energy.
Not rushing anyone.
Making math puzzles feel like a warm cup of tea.
Knowledgeable without being preachy.

---

## Target Audience

- Adults 40+ seeking brain exercises
- Seniors wanting cognitive sharpness
- Puzzle lovers who've mastered Sudoku
- People who remember the satisfaction of the Sunday crossword
- Anyone seeking screen-free mental stimulation

---

## Tone

Educational but warm.
Casual authority.
Like a patient friend explaining over coffee.

Never condescending.
Never rushed.
Conversational, not textbook.

---

## Writing Style

### Structure
- Short, punchy sentences for key concepts
- Line breaks for rhythm and breathing room
- Bullet lists for options and steps
- Bold for **key terms** only
- Numbered steps for processes

### Voice
- Direct second person—always "you"
- Personal, conversational
- Gentle humor when appropriate
- Forward momentum at section ends

### Rhythm Pattern
```
Statement.
Expansion.

Short. Punchy. Clear.

Then a longer sentence that brings it together and gives context.
```

## What Makes This Voice Work

**Reassuring authority.**
Not showing off—just sharing what works.

**Anti-intimidation.**
Every "complex" thing gets broken down.

**Patient pacing.**
Repeat key concepts. Give breathing room.

**Practical focus.**
Every explanation leads to action.

**Respect for the reader.**
Assumes intelligence. Never talks down.
Just assumes they haven't done *this* puzzle before.
