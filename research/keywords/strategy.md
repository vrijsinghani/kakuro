## SEO Recommendations

1. **Title:** Lead with "Kakuro" + difficulty level + demographic
   - Example: *"Large Print Kakuro for Seniors: Easy to Medium Puzzles for Brain Health"*

2. **Subtitle:** Include secondary keywords
   - Example: *"Cross Sums Logic Games with Solutions - Big Print Number Puzzles"*

3. **A+ Content:** Visually prove "Large Print" claim with interior previews
