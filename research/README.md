# Market Research

This directory contains all market research, competitive analysis, and strategic planning documents for the Kakuro KDP publishing venture.

## Directory Structure

### `competitors/`
Analysis of existing Kakuro puzzle books on Amazon.

**What to track:**
- Book titles and authors
- Pricing strategies
- Page counts and puzzle counts
- Customer reviews (ratings, common complaints, praise)
- Cover designs and interior previews
- Sales rank (BSR - Best Sellers Rank)
- Publication dates
- Series vs. standalone books

**Files:**
- `competitor_analysis.xlsx` - Spreadsheet tracking competitors
- `top_sellers.md` - Notes on best-selling books
- `screenshots/` - Cover and interior preview screenshots
- `reviews_analysis.md` - Summary of customer feedback patterns

**Key metrics to monitor:**
- Average price point by difficulty level
- Typical puzzle count (100, 200, 500, 1000+)
- Most common trim sizes
- Review sentiment (what customers love/hate)

### `keywords/`
Keyword research for Amazon SEO and discoverability.

**Primary keywords (from research):**
- "Kakuro puzzle books"
- "Kakuro for adults"
- "Kakuro puzzles large print"
- "Kakuro puzzle books for beginners"
- "Logic puzzles Kakuro"
- "Japanese number puzzles"

**Research tools:**
- Amazon search autocomplete
- Google Keyword Planner
- Book Bolt keyword research
- Publisher Rocket
- KDP Rocket

**Files:**
- `primary_keywords.md` - Main target keywords
- `long_tail_keywords.md` - Specific, lower-competition phrases
- `seasonal_keywords.md` - Holiday/seasonal variations
- `keyword_performance.xlsx` - Track which keywords drive sales

**Keyword strategy:**
- Title: Include primary keyword (e.g., "Kakuro Puzzle Book for Adults")
- Subtitle: Include 2-3 secondary keywords
- Backend keywords: Use all 7 keyword slots (max 50 characters each)
- Description: Natural keyword integration

### `pricing/`
Pricing strategy and profitability analysis.

**Factors to consider:**
- KDP printing costs (varies by page count, trim size, paper type)
- Competitive pricing in the niche
- Perceived value (puzzle count, quality, features)
- Royalty rates (35% vs. 70% - depends on price range)
- Target profit margin

**Files:**
- `pricing_calculator.xlsx` - Calculate costs and profits
- `competitive_pricing.md` - Competitor price analysis
- `pricing_strategy.md` - Recommended pricing by book type
- `royalty_scenarios.xlsx` - Compare different price points

**Pricing guidelines:**
- Beginner books (100-200 puzzles): $6.99 - $9.99
- Standard books (300-500 puzzles): $9.99 - $12.99
- Large collections (1000+ puzzles): $14.99 - $19.99
- Large print editions: +$2-3 premium
- Expert/challenging: +$1-2 premium

**KDP royalty tiers:**
- 35% royalty: Any price
- 70% royalty: $2.99 - $9.99 (US marketplace)
- Consider 70% tier for maximum profit on lower-priced books

### `trends/`
Market trends and opportunity analysis.

**What to track:**
- Search volume trends (Google Trends, Amazon)
- Seasonal patterns (puzzle books often spike in winter, holidays)
- Emerging sub-niches (themed Kakuro, educational variants)
- Competitor launch patterns
- Customer demand signals (reviews, questions)

**Files:**
- `search_trends.md` - Google Trends data and analysis
- `seasonal_patterns.md` - When to launch books for maximum impact
- `opportunity_gaps.md` - Underserved niches and opportunities
- `market_size.md` - Overall market size and growth estimates

**Key insights from initial research:**
- Monthly search volume: ~36,635 for Kakuro-related terms
- Competition: Exceptionally low compared to Sudoku/Crosswords
- Market gap: High demand + low supply = blue ocean opportunity
- Best launch timing: September-November (holiday shopping season)

## Research Workflow

### 1. Competitive Analysis
- Search Amazon for "Kakuro puzzle book"
- Analyze top 20-30 results
- Document pricing, features, reviews
- Identify gaps and opportunities

### 2. Keyword Research
- Use Amazon autocomplete for suggestions
- Check Google Trends for search volume
- Analyze competitor keywords (in titles, descriptions)
- Build list of primary and long-tail keywords

### 3. Pricing Strategy
- Calculate KDP printing costs for target specs
- Analyze competitor pricing
- Determine optimal price point for profit and competitiveness
- Test different price points after launch

### 4. Trend Monitoring
- Weekly: Check BSR of competitors
- Monthly: Review search trends
- Quarterly: Comprehensive market analysis
- Ongoing: Monitor customer reviews for insights
