# Kakuro Pricing & Profitability Strategy

> Source: Gemini Deep Research (Jan 2026)

## KDP Printing Cost Structure (2025)

### Standard Trim (6" x 9")
- Fixed Cost: ~$1.00/unit
- Per Page: ~$0.012/page

### Large Trim (8.5" x 11")
- Fixed Cost: ~$0.85-$1.00/unit
- Per Page: ~$0.012/page

**Formula:** `Fixed Cost + (Page Count × Per Page Cost) = Print Cost`

---

## Cost Scenarios (8.5x11, B&W)

| Pages | Est. Print Cost |
|-------|-----------------|
| 120 | $2.30 - $2.85 |
| 300 | $4.60 - $5.45 |

---

## Royalty Comparison

**Formula:** `(List Price × 60%) - Print Cost = Net Royalty`

### Strategy 1: "Volume" (300 Pages)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Target Price | $12.99 |
| Print Cost | ~$4.85 |
| Royalty | **$2.94/book** |

**Pros:** High perceived value, justifies premium  
**Cons:** Lower margin %, higher risk if price drops

### Strategy 2: "Series" (120 Pages) ⭐ RECOMMENDED

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Target Price | $7.99 |
| Print Cost | ~$2.45 |
| Royalty | **$2.34/book** |

**Pros:**
- Lower barrier to entry for customers
- Multiple volumes = higher lifetime value
- Higher BSR velocity (lower price = more sales)
- Multiple "new release" algorithm boosts

**Cons:** Lower absolute profit per unit

---

## Competitor Pricing Insights

- *The Ultimate Brain Health Puzzle Book* lists at **$12.99** but trades at **~$8.43**
- Niche beginner books maintain **~$5.95** for impulse buys

**Takeaway:** List at $12.99 for perceived value; expect street price to settle lower.

---

## Recommendation for New Entrant

> **The Series Strategy is superior.**
> 
> A 120-page book at **$7.99-$8.99** enables:
> - Faster publication velocity
> - A "shelf" of products (Vol 1, 2, 3...)
> - Maximum algorithm boost from multiple new releases
