### Black Ink, 110-828 Pages

| Trim Size | Fixed Cost | Per Page |
|-----------|------------|----------|
| Regular (≤6.12" × ≤9") | $1.00 | $0.012 |
| **Large (8.5" × 11")** | $1.00 | **$0.017** |

**Formula:** `Fixed Cost + (Page Count × Per Page Cost) = Print Cost`

> ⚠️ **Important:** Large trim is defined as width >6.12" OR height >9".
> Our 8.5" × 11" books are LARGE TRIM and use the $0.017/page rate!

## Royalty Comparison

**Formula:** `(List Price × 60%) - Print Cost = Net Royalty`

### Strategy 1: "Volume" (327 Pages, Large Trim)

| List Price | Gross (60%) | Print Cost | **Royalty** |
|------------|-------------|------------|-------------|
| $12.99 | $7.79 | $6.56 | **$1.23** |
| $14.99 | $8.99 | $6.56 | **$2.43** |
| $16.99 | $10.19 | $6.56 | **$3.63** |
| $19.99 | $11.99 | $6.56 | **$5.43** |

**Pros:** High perceived value, premium positioning
**Cons:** Low margin at $12.99, need higher price for profitability

### Strategy 2: "Series" (120 Pages, Large Trim)

| List Price | Gross (60%) | Print Cost | **Royalty** |
|------------|-------------|------------|-------------|
| $7.99 | $4.79 | $3.04 | **$1.75** |
| $8.99 | $5.39 | $3.04 | **$2.35** |
| $9.99 | $5.99 | $3.04 | **$2.95** |

**Pros:**
- Lower barrier to entry for customers
- Multiple volumes = higher lifetime value
- Higher BSR velocity (lower price = more sales)
- Multiple "new release" algorithm boosts

**Cons:** Lower absolute profit per unit

## Competitor Pricing Insights

- *The Ultimate Brain Health Puzzle Book* lists at **$12.99** but trades at **~$8.43**
- Niche beginner books maintain **~$5.95** for impulse buys

**Takeaway:** For 300+ page large-trim books, price at **$14.99-$16.99** to achieve meaningful royalties ($2.43-$3.63/book).

---

## Recommendation for Large-Trim Books

> **For 300+ page books at 8.5" × 11":**
>
> Price at **$14.99 minimum** to achieve ~$2.43 royalty per sale.
> Consider **$16.99** for premium positioning (~$3.63 royalty).
>
> **For series strategy:**
>
> A 120-page book at **$8.99** enables:
> - $2.35 royalty per book
> - Faster publication velocity
> - Multiple "new release" algorithm boosts
