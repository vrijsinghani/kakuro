# Chapter 5: Building Skills
*7x7 Grids - Expanding Your Vision*

Now that you've warmed up, we're stepping up to 7x7 grids. These puzzles offer slightly more room to maneuver, but also more opportunities for complex interactions.

**What's new:**
*   Longer runs (3-4 cells).
*   More intersecting clues.
*   You'll need to use the elimination method more frequently.
