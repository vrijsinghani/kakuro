# Conduct Market Research

## Summary
Populate the `research/` directory with market research data per the framework in `research/README.md`. This research will validate book positioning, pricing, and keywords before KDP launch.

## Research Areas

### 1. Competitors (`research/competitors/`)
Analyze top Kakuro books on Amazon:
- Top 20-30 results for "Kakuro puzzle book"
- Document: titles, prices, page counts, puzzle counts, BSR, reviews
- Identify gaps and opportunities

**Deliverables:**
- `top_sellers.md` - Analysis of best-selling books
- `reviews_analysis.md` - Common praise/complaints from customers

### 2. Keywords (`research/keywords/`)
Build keyword list for Amazon SEO:
- Amazon search autocomplete analysis
- Competitor title/description keywords
- Primary vs. long-tail keywords

**Deliverables:**
- `primary_keywords.md` - Main target keywords
- `long_tail_keywords.md` - Specific, lower-competition phrases

### 3. Pricing (`research/pricing/`)
Determine optimal price point:
- KDP printing cost calculation for our specs
- Competitor pricing analysis
- Royalty scenario comparison

**Deliverables:**
- `pricing_strategy.md` - Recommended price with justification
- `competitive_pricing.md` - Competitor price comparison

### 4. Trends (`research/trends/`)
Analyze market opportunity:
- Google Trends data
- Seasonal patterns
- Market gaps/opportunities

**Deliverables:**
- `search_trends.md` - Search volume and trends
- `opportunity_gaps.md` - Underserved niches

## Verification
- All 8 deliverable files created
- Research informs final book metadata (title, subtitle, price)
